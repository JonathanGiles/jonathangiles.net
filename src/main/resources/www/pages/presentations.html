Presentations

Over the years I’ve given many presentations all around the world, from the US to Europe, and India to Japan. I love interacting with like-minded developers, and I have been recognised as a ‘JavaOne Rockstar’ for my popular presentations at JavaOne. Unfortunately I’ve never kept records of these presentations, but I’ve searched Google and my file system to link to as many as I can on this page. I hope they are helpful!
